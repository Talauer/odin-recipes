Argentinian Beef Empanadas

Traditional Argentinian recipe

Description

Beef empanadas are an icon of Argentinian cuisine. Made with ground beef, onions, hard-boiled eggs, and olives, wrapped in homemade dough and baked in the oven.

Ingredients (for 12 empanadas)

For the dough:

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 100g butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 200ml warm water

For the filling:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 large onions
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs
  • 12 pitted green olives
  • 2 tablespoons sweet paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 100g raisins (optional)

Steps

  1. For the dough: mix flour with salt, add cubed butter and work until crumbly.
  2. Add warm water gradually until forming a homogeneous dough. Let rest for 30 minutes.
  3. For the filling: finely chop onions and saut√© in oil until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  5. Add paprika, cumin, salt and pepper. Cook for 10 more minutes and let cool.
  6. Add chopped hard-boiled eggs, olives and raisins if using.
  7. Roll out dough, cut circles and place a tablespoon of filling in each.
  8. Close forming the traditional Argentinian crimped edge.
  9. Brush with beaten egg and bake at 200¬∞C for 20-25 minutes until golden.
