Vegetable Stir Fry

Description

A quick and healthy vegetable stir fry with colorful veggies in a savory sauce, perfect for a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
  2. Add carrots and broccoli, stir fry for 3 minutes.
  3. Add bell pepper and snap peas, cook for 2 more minutes.
  4. Add garlic and ginger, cook until fragrant.
  5. Mix soy sauce, honey, and sesame oil in a small bowl.
  6. Pour sauce over vegetables and toss to coat.
  7. Cook for 1-2 minutes until sauce thickens slightly.
  8. Serve over rice or noodles.
