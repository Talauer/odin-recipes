Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish from Rome made with eggs, hard cheese, cured pork, and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or guanciale
  • 3 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. Dice pancetta and cook until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs with grated cheese and black pepper.
  4. Drain pasta, reserving some pasta water.
  5. Combine hot pasta with pancetta, then mix in egg mixture.
  6. Add pasta water if needed to create creamy sauce.
  7. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.
